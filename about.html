Our Heritage

A Culinary Legacy Since 1998

For over 25 years, Malabar Spice has been dedicated to preserving the rich culinary heritage of Kerala. Our journey began with a simple passion: to share the authentic flavors and traditions passed down through generations.

From the vibrant spice markets to the tranquil backwaters, every element of Kerala's culture is infused into our dishes. Our founder, a master of traditional Malabar cuisine, curated our menu with a commitment to quality and authenticity.

Since 1998, we have been a cornerstone of Thiruvananthapuram's dining scene, celebrating the region's unique culinary soul.

Our Promise

Culinary Excellence & Sourcing

Only the Finest Ingredients

We believe that true flavor begins with the freshest, most ethical sourcing. We partner with local spice farmers and backwater fishermen to ensure that every ingredient, from the peppercorns to the seafood, is of the highest quality.

Our kitchen follows traditional methods - slow cooking, stone grinding, and hand-picking produce - preserving the integrity of each dish as it was meant to be tasted.

Our Space

Creating Memories, One Table at a Time

Beyond our menu, Malabar Spice is a sanctuary for communal dining and celebration. Our space is designed to transport you, blending traditional architectural elements with modern comfort.

From the intimate lighting to the intricate wood carvings, every detail tells a story. Whether you are celebrating a special occasion, hosting a corporate event, or enjoying a casual dinner, we invite you to experience the authentic warmth and hospitality of Kerala.
